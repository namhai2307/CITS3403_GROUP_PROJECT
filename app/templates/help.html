Making Your Account

To create your account, you will need to enter three things.

  1. A username: Anything you like! This is how you will find your connections to share your calendar
  2. A valid email address!
  3. A password to secure your account, with the following requirements:
    • Be between 12 to 128 characters in length.
    • Contain at least one lowercase letter.
    • Contain at least one uppercase letter.
    • Contain at least one number.

Changing Your Details

You can update your account details, such as your username, email address, or password, by visiting the Profile page. Simply edit the relevant fields and submit the form to save your changes. Make sure to follow the password requirements if you choose to update your password. Your updated information will take effect immediately, and you will see a confirmation message once the changes are successful.

Adding An Event

You can add new events to your calendar using the Dashboard tab.
Using the "Add Event" form on the left-hand side of the page. Simply fill in the details for your event, then submit to see it added to your calendar on the right.

  • Start and End Time: The time period during which the event will occur. End time currently must be on the same day as the start.
  • Title: The header for your event that will display beneath the calendar when viewing the day.
  • Description: A short description that will be displayed beneath the Title when viewing the day.
  • Privacy Level: Decide who can view details of this event: Just yourself, specific friends, or all friends.
Events on a given day will be displayed beneath the calendar when you click on the appropriate date.

Editing an Event

After creating a new event, events on the day you currently have selected in the calendar will gain a button to either "Edit" or "Delete" the event.
"Edit" will open a window within the app to change any of the details for the event. Once you are happy with the new details, simply submit the form and your calendar will be updated. If you have started editing the wrong event, simply save without changing or click the X in the rop right of the window.
"Delete" will open a banner to confirm that you are are sure you would like to proceed with this action. If you are, simply click "OK" and the event will be removed from your calendar.

Why is my Calendar Red?

Your calendar uses a heatmap color scheme to visually represent how busy each day is. Days that are less busy will appear in cooler colors like green, while busier days will shift to warmer colors such as yellow, orange, or red. The more hours you have scheduled on a day, the warmer and more intense the color will appear, making it easy to spot your busiest days at a glance.

Adding A Contact

Send a friend request to your contacts by searching their username on the Profile tab! The users that best match your search term will be displayed beneath the box, so try to be as close to their username as you can. Then it's simply a matter of clicking the "Add Friend" button to ship off the request for them to review.
If another user has sent you a friend request, these will appear just beneath the search box when you enter your profile. Simply click "Accept" if you want to link your account to the other user, or "Delete" if you do not.

Using Chat

On the Chat page you can send messages to your friends right here in the app! Select your friend in the dropdown menu on the left-hand side of the screen, then your conversation will appear in the main box. Chat like you would on any other service, typing your messages in the field beneath the chat history.

Viewing a Friend's Calendar

In the Visualisation tab you can view the calendars of your friends by selecting their username from the dropdown menu at the top. Don't worry about privacy, only events they have decided are public will be visible.
